Smart E-Waste Reusability, Forecasting and Sustainability Recommendation Platform
Project Overview
The Smart E-Waste Reusability, Forecasting and Sustainability Recommendation Platform is designed for private e-waste recycling organizations to improve product identification, reusability assessment, e-waste forecasting, sustainability planning, and infrastructure recommendations.
The platform combines Computer Vision (CV), forecasting models, recommendation systems, and sustainability analytics to support efficient recycling operations and strategic decision-making.
Objectives
Primary Objectives


Forecast future e-waste generation.

Identify electronic products using Computer Vision.

Assess product reusability and recyclability.

Estimate reusable and e-waste value.

Generate sustainability recommendations.

Support infrastructure planning for recyclers.

Improve logistics and collection efficiency.
