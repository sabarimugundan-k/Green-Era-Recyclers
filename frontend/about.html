Our Story

From a Green Dream to Reality

Green Era Recyclers was established in 2017 as an authorized e-waste recycling company committed to environmental sustainability and responsible waste management. What started with a vision to tackle the growing e-waste crisis has grown into a full-scale recycling ecosystem serving thousands of customers across Tamil Nadu.

Our journey has been driven by a simple belief: that technology and sustainability can work hand in hand. By leveraging AI and machine learning, we've revolutionized how e-waste is assessed, processed, and recycled.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2017 — The Beginning

Green Era Recyclers established in Coimbatore with a vision to tackle the growing e-waste crisis through responsible recycling and circular economy principles.

2018 — Authorized Operations

Received authorization as an e-waste recycler and set up recycling operations at our head office facility in Edayarpalayam, Coimbatore.

2019 — Public Awareness

Conducted large-scale public awareness campaigns and environmental exhibitions across Tamil Nadu on electronic waste management.

2020 — First Collection Bins

Installed Tamil Nadu's first dedicated public e-waste collection bin initiative. Launched community collection drives.

2021 — ISO Certifications

Achieved ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018 certifications. Established partnerships with industries and institutions.

2022 — AI Integration

Launched proprietary AI assessment platform for automated e-waste valuation, sorting, and intelligent recycling recommendations.

2023 — Expansion

Expanded collection center network across Coimbatore with 7 dedicated hubs. Partnered with 200+ corporate clients.

2024 — Digital Transformation

Launched comprehensive digital platform for pickup management, recycling tracking, and customer engagement across Tamil Nadu.

2025 — Industry Leader

Recognized as a leading e-waste recycler in Tamil Nadu. Expanded service coverage to major cities across the state.
