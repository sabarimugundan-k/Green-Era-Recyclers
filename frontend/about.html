Our Story

From a Green Dream to Reality

GreenEra was founded in 2020 by a group of environmental engineers and technology experts who recognized the growing challenge of electronic waste. What started as a small community initiative has grown into a full-scale e-waste management platform serving thousands of customers.

Our journey has been driven by a simple belief: that technology and sustainability can work hand in hand. By leveraging AI and machine learning, we've revolutionized how e-waste is assessed, processed, and recycled.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2020 — The Beginning

GreenEra founded with a vision to tackle the growing e-waste crisis. First community collection drive launched.

2021 — First Facility

Opened our first recycling facility in Eco City. Processed 5,000 devices in the first year.

2022 — AI Integration

Launched our proprietary AI assessment platform, enabling automated valuation and sorting of e-waste.

2023 — Expansion

Expanded to 3 facilities across the region. Partnered with 200+ corporate clients.

2024 — Industry Leader

Recognized as top e-waste management innovator. Certified zero-landfill operations.

2025 — Global Reach

International expansion begins. Serving 25+ cities with comprehensive e-waste solutions.

Our Foundation

Vision, Mission & Goals

Our Vision

To create a world where no electronic waste ends up in landfills, establishing a fully circular economy for all electronic products.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, AI-powered e-waste management solutions that maximize material recovery and minimize environmental impact.

Our Goals

  • 1M devices recycled by 2027
  • 50% reduction in e-waste landfill
  • 100% renewable energy operations
  • Expand to 100+ cities globally
